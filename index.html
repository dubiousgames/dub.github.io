Origin

How DEEB
came to be.

DEEB started in 2025 as two friends who wanted to make games. The name came from taking the word deep and flipping the p into a b — simple, memorable, ours.

We build everything ourselves — code, art, sound, story. No big team, no big budget. Just two people making the games we want to play.

Our games are split across two completely separate universes — one built in Godot, one in RPG Maker. Both are handcrafted with their own lore and stories. They have nothing to do with each other, and that's intentional.
