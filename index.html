STUDIO_MANIFESTO
01

Casual Gameplay: Every project must contain a minimum of 6 hours of content.

02

Earnable Advantages: Microtransactions are only permitted if they can be earned through regular gameplay.

03

Skill Over Pay: Any purchased item is automatically given a slight debuff and can never be the best in the game.

04

No Cash-to-Currency: In-game currencies absolutely cannot be bought with real-world money.

05

Privacy First: No ID or face verification allowed. Phone numbers are the only permitted verification method.

[ THE DUBIOUS GUARANTEE ]

We hold ourselves to these standards. If a project fails to meet 1 to 3 of these rules, the game will be discounted as an apology to our players.

If a project violates 4 or more rules, that game will be released for free.

In the event that a project breaks every single rule, we will issue a formal public apology and apply a penalty discount to our entire catalog. We stand by our work.
